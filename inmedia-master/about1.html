About Farmhub





Farmhub

is a web application that focuses on providing trusted communication between farmers, provincial sellers and local retailers.

This development shall focus on those individuals who shares common necessities for their businesses. Here are the following audiences:

  • Local Business Owners (Provinces of Philippines)
    • - This includes the local farmers, livestock sellers, and fisheries.
  • Local Market / Retailers (Cities of Metro Manila)
    • - This includes big and small markets with legitimate certifications to run a business inside the jurisdiction of Metro Manila.
  • Advertising Companies
    • - This only scopes the related ventures, products, and services align to the goal of the application.
